Chapter 8 Glossary

Acquisition: willing seller enters into a purchase agreement and sells the title of a property to a land trust or government.

Area of Interest: the geographic area that will be assessed for conservation value.

Easement: limits certain uses of the land in order to achieve particular conservation objectives while keeping the land in the owner’s control

Goal: Conservation target

Goals and Objectives:

  • Habitat Goal: Restore and Conserve Habitat

  • Objective: Restore and conserve the health, diversity, and resilience of key coastal, estuarine, and marine habitats.

  • Water Quality and Quantity Goal: Restore Water Quality and Quantity

  • Objective: Restore and protect the water quality and quantity of the Gulf Coast region’s fresh, estuarine, and marine waters.

Living Coastal and Marine Resource Goal: Replenish and Protect Living Coastal and Marine Resources

Objective: Restore and protect healthy, diverse, and sustainable living coastal and marine resources.

Community Resilience Goal: Enhance Community Resilience

Objective: Build upon and sustain communities with capacity to adapt to short- and long-term changes.

Gulf Economy Goal: Restore and Revitalize the Gulf Economy

Objective: Enhance the sustainability and resiliency of the Gulf economy.

Priority Attribute: key features that more specifically define the goal

Measures: quantifiable characterization of priority attribute; x-axis of the utility function.

Stewardship: Land management, restoration, and maintenance

Weights: the relative importance that is placed on each measure, priority attribute, and/or goal

Utility functions: method by which each measure is represented as a score. (i.e. relationship)

Score: a normalized value from 0-1 representing preference.
